  About me
 
 

I'm a Principal Data Scientist at Automatic Data Processing (ADP), LLC since 2017, specializing in predictive modeling for client retention purpose. I have been with Wireless Big Data Analytics Research of Huawei R&D in USA site where I focused on algorithm research and development. I hold Ph.D degree of Electrical Engineering from Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at New Jersey Institute of Technology under supervision of Prof. Akansu, Master degree from College of Science and Engineering at Central Michigan University, and Bachelor degree of Electrical Engineering from Department of Electrical and Electric Engineering at Shanghai Institute of Technology, China.

My research interests include facial emotion recognition, machine learning, data mining, and their applications in various data driven domains such as finance, psychology, health care, etc.. My relevant work has been interviewed by Wall Street Journal and Forbes. He also involves in research on big data analytics and quantitative modeling for wireless networks, with a focus on wireless network performance, traffic patterns, user behaviors, and QoS.
